The Exhibition
Discover new sides of 68 K-pop artists from 9 groups. The Exhibition consists of three sections. The first section features
colorful portraits with handwritten messages. The second section showcases black-and-white portraits expressing artistic
emotions. Finally, the third section presents group and individual photos. All the photos are exclusive and cannot be found
anywhere else.
|
The Movie
A spectacular stage never seen before, even in music videos. Exclusive footage of performances by 9 K-pop groups on virtual
stages created using cutting-edge XR filming technology will be unveiled. In particular, BTS performed their hit song "DNA"
on this stage. You can enjoy a 45-minute highlight version, including performances on LED stages.
|
The Experience
Experience D'Festa with K-pop stars. A space is set up where you can immerse yourself in the 3D LED stage filmed by K-pop
stars. One side is designed with a mirror, making it the perfect spot for taking selfies. Additionally, you can take photos in
various poses with 68 K-pop stars at our Photo booth.
|
Artist Visits / Rising Stars
Many K-pop stars visited The Source Mall to celebrate the opening of D'Festa in Los Angeles. Stars such as Rowoon from boy
group SF9, who is now active as an actor, TWS, NMIXX, P1Harmony, ZB1, G-Idle, and STAYC attended the event. There is
growing anticipation about which K-pop star will visit next.
|
